Brisbane, Queesnland, Australia

Brisbane is a beautiful city with many bridges (for instance the Story Bridge) that cross the beutiful Bribane River.
The city also has botanic gardens and tall scycrapers.
You can take the CityCat ferries to different places. The ferries are included in the public transport.
I like this city because it is very different from my home city. One difference being in the number of inhabitants.
In my hometown we have about 60.000 inhabitants whereas in Brisbane there are about 2.3 milloin inhabitants.
Some interesting places are Lone Pine Coala Sanctuary, Sea world, Sounth Bank and Mount Coot-Tha.
Brisbane is located at the east coast and is close to many places like Surfer's Paradise, The Great Barrier Reef and many islands which might be worth visiting.
About 2h's drive from Brisbane is also Cape Byron which is the Most Easterly Point of the Australian Mainland.
